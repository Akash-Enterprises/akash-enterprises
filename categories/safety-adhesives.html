Safety, Floor Marking & Adhesives

Safety marking and adhesive products help define movement, reduce accidents, and protect work areas in industrial and commercial environments. Proper selection supports compliance, visibility, and long-term surface performance.

How to Choose the Right Safety or Adhesive Product

  • Identify whether the requirement is visual guidance, slip resistance, or surface protection.
  • Check adhesive strength based on floor type, surface finish, and expected foot or vehicle traffic.
  • Select visibility level and color coding according to workplace safety practices.
  • Consider indoor vs outdoor exposure, moisture, and cleaning routines.

Common Buyer Mistakes

  • Applying tapes on unclean or uneven surfaces, reducing adhesion life.
  • Using low-visibility tapes in high-risk or poorly lit areas.
  • Selecting general-purpose adhesives for heavy-duty industrial floors.
  • Ignoring wear rates in high-traffic zones.

Primary Use Cases

  • Factory floors, warehouses, and production units
  • Walkways, hazard zones, and restricted areas
  • Temporary surface protection during construction or maintenance
  • Safety marking for equipment, vehicles, and loading areas
