Agro-Textiles & Landscaping

Agro-textiles play a critical role in modern farming and landscaping by controlling sunlight, managing ground conditions, and improving long-term crop and surface protection. The right selection directly impacts plant health, maintenance effort, and operational efficiency.

How to Choose the Right Agro-Textile

  • Identify whether your priority is sunlight control, weed suppression, or physical protection.
  • Match shade percentage or fabric density to the specific crop or application.
  • Consider outdoor exposure factors such as wind load, UV resistance, and installation method.
  • Ensure compatibility between nets, clips, and support structures for long-term stability.

Common Buyer Mistakes

  • Selecting shade nets based only on price without considering UV life and GSM.
  • Using improper fastening methods, leading to tearing or early failure.
  • Installing weed mats without proper ground preparation, reducing effectiveness.
  • Over-shading crops that require controlled but sufficient sunlight.

Primary Use Cases

  • Nurseries, polyhouses, and open-field crop protection
  • Landscaping projects for residential and commercial properties
  • Weed control in gardens, pathways, and greenhouse floors
  • Temporary or permanent shading for outdoor work areas
