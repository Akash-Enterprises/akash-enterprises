About Akash Enterprises

A trusted trading and distribution partner for industrial, packaging, and agro-textile products — serving businesses and individuals across India.

Who We Are

Akash Enterprises is a B2B and retail trading company specialising in industrial materials, packaging solutions, and agro-textiles. We operate as a direct supply partner for manufacturers, contractors, logistics operators, farmers, and retailers who need reliable materials delivered on time.

We stock and supply 34 products across 6 categories — from heavy-duty HDPE tarpaulins and mooring ropes to BOPP packing tapes, agro shade nets, and industrial LDPE sheets. Every product in our catalogue is selected for proven performance in Indian field conditions.

Our operations are rooted in Kerala with pan-India supply capability. We serve buyers from Tier 1 metro warehouses to remote agricultural districts — wherever the material is needed.

What We Supply

Agro-Textiles

How We Operate

A structured process that eliminates product mismatches and ensures you receive exactly what your application requires.

01

Understand the Requirement

We ask about your application, usage conditions, load requirements, and delivery location before recommending any product. Generic suggestions cause procurement errors — we avoid them.

02

Recommend the Right Product

Based on your requirement, we recommend suitable product options — including GSM, dimensions, material type, and quantity — with clear reasoning for each choice.

03

Confirm Pricing & Availability

We confirm current pricing, stock availability, and realistic delivery timelines before any commitment is made on either side.

04

Coordinate Dispatch & Follow Up

Once confirmed, we coordinate dispatch and stay in communication until the material reaches you. Bulk orders receive priority handling.
