About Akash Enterprises

35 Years of Quality You Can Trust

Since February 1991, Akash Enterprises has been a trusted partner in the industrial textile and packaging industry. We started with a simple promise: to provide materials that don't fail when you need them most. Today, we are recognised as one of Kerala's leading suppliers of heavy-duty plastics, packaging, and industrial textiles.

We Don't Just Sell Products — We Provide Solutions

Most suppliers just ship an order. At Akash Enterprises, we make sure you get the complete solution for your job. Whether you are covering a fleet of trucks, lining a fish pond, or packing goods for export, we don't just give you a product; we give you the right tools to get the job done right the first time.

What We Supply

Six product verticals covering the core material needs of industrial, agricultural, and commercial operations in India.

Industrial Tarpaulins

Toughened waterproof covers for trucks, machinery, and monsoon protection — HDPE, canvas, cross-lamination, nylon, and geo membrane variants.

Agro-Textiles

UV-stabilised shade nets (50%–90% shade) to keep crops safe, cool, and protected — plus weed mats, agro clips, vehicle covers, and rain ponchos.

Packaging & Logistics

Reliable protection for goods in transit — bubble wrap, corrugated paper rolls, stretch film, BOPP tapes, woven sacks, and strapping kits.

Ropes & Cordage

High-tensile Nylon and PP ropes designed for heavy lifting, marine use, and secure bundling — plus PP twine, nylon thread, and lashing belts.

Safety & Adhesives

High-visibility floor marking tapes, specialised tarpaulin repair tapes, caution tape, reflective tape, anti-skid tape, and masking tape.

Industrial Sheets

Heavy-duty plastic sheets for flooring and high-density waterproof liners for Biofloc tanks or ponds — LDPE, PVC clear sheets, cutting boards, and eyelets.

How We Operate

A structured process that eliminates product mismatches and ensures you receive exactly what your application requires.

01

Understand the Requirement

We ask about your application, usage conditions, load requirements, and delivery location before recommending any product. Generic suggestions cause procurement errors — we avoid them.

02

Recommend the Right Product

Based on your requirement, we recommend suitable product options — including GSM, dimensions, material type, and quantity — with clear reasoning for each choice.

03

Confirm Pricing & Availability

We confirm current pricing, stock availability, and realistic delivery timelines before any commitment is made on either side.

04

Coordinate Dispatch & Follow Up

Once confirmed, we coordinate dispatch and stay in communication until the material reaches you. Bulk orders receive priority handling.
