LDPE Sheets & Rolls

Flexible and waterproof LDPE sheets designed for packaging, lining, surface protection, and multiple industrial applications.

Product Overview

LDPE Sheets & Rolls are widely used for packaging, lining, moisture protection, and surface covering applications. They provide excellent flexibility and resistance to water and chemicals.

Manufactured from low-density polyethylene, these sheets are lightweight, durable, and easy to handle, making them suitable for both commercial and industrial usage.

Key Features

  • Flexible and lightweight material
  • Waterproof and moisture resistant
  • Good chemical resistance
  • Easy to cut, fold, and install
  • Available in roll form for convenience

Specifications

  • Material: LDPE (Low Density Polyethylene)
  • Form: Sheets / Rolls
  • Thickness: Varies as per requirement
  • Colour: Transparent / Natural
  • Usage: Packaging and protection

Applications & Use Cases

  • Industrial and commercial packaging
  • Moisture and vapor barriers
  • Surface and floor protection
  • Lining for containers and storage areas
  • General-purpose plastic applications

Best Suited For

  • Packaging of goods requiring moisture protection
  • Factories and warehouses needing flexible lining material
  • Temporary surface protection during storage or transport

Selection Note

For Indian operating conditions, thickness selection should be based on handling frequency and load. Thicker LDPE sheets are recommended where repeated folding, dragging, or exposure to sharp edges is expected.

Commonly Used Along With

  • BOPP Packing Tapes for sealing
  • Bubble Wrap or Foam Sheets for cushioning
  • Floor Protection Sheets for site work

Product FAQs

  • Which thickness of LDPE sheet should be selected?
    Thickness depends on application and handling. Thicker sheets are recommended for repeated use, heavy loads, or rough industrial handling.
  • Are LDPE sheets waterproof?
    Yes. LDPE sheets provide effective waterproofing and moisture protection for packaging, lining, and surface covering applications.
  • Can LDPE sheets be used outdoors?
    LDPE sheets can be used for short to medium-term outdoor protection, but prolonged UV exposure may reduce lifespan unless specified otherwise.
  • Are LDPE sheets suitable for food-related packaging?
    Food-grade LDPE sheets can be used for food packaging and lining applications when specified and handled correctly.
  • Can LDPE rolls be cut to required sizes on site?
    Yes. LDPE rolls are easy to cut manually and can be customized to required dimensions during installation or packing.
  • Are bulk quantities available for industrial buyers?
    Yes. LDPE sheets and rolls are available in bulk quantities for factories, warehouses, and project-based requirements.
