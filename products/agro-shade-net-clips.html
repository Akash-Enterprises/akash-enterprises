Agro Shade Net Clips

Strong, UV-stabilized plastic clips designed for secure and long-lasting fastening of agro shade nets to support structures.

Product Overview

Agro Shade Net Clips are used to securely attach shade nets to wires, pipes, or frames. Manufactured using UV-stabilized plastic, these clips ensure long-term outdoor durability without damaging the net fabric.

Key Features

  • Strong locking mechanism
  • UV stabilized for outdoor use
  • Reusable and easy to install
  • Prevents tearing of shade nets
  • Compatible with most agro nets

Specifications

  • Material: UV Stabilized Plastic
  • Colour: Black / Green
  • Design: Snap-fit locking
  • Usage: Outdoor agricultural structures

Applications & Use Cases

  • Greenhouses and polyhouses
  • Nurseries and shade houses
  • Agricultural net installations
  • Horticulture and landscaping

Best Suited For

  • Permanent and semi-permanent shade net installations
  • Areas exposed to wind where secure fastening is critical
  • Installations requiring repeated net removal and re-fixing

Selection Note

Clip size and spacing should be selected based on net GSM, wind conditions, and support structure spacing.

Product FAQs

  • How many clips are required per square meter of shade net?
    Clip quantity depends on wind conditions and net GSM. Typically, clips are installed at 30–45 cm intervals along edges and support lines for secure fastening.
  • Are these clips compatible with all shade net GSMs?
    Yes. These clips are designed to work with most standard agro shade nets, including higher GSM nets used in permanent installations.
  • Can the clips be reused after removing the shade net?
    Yes. The snap-fit design allows repeated installation and removal without damaging the clip or the net.
  • Do shade net clips withstand Indian outdoor conditions?
    Yes. The clips are UV stabilized and suitable for prolonged exposure to sunlight, heat, and monsoon conditions across India.
  • Are these clips suitable for high-wind areas?
    Yes, when used with proper spacing and support structures, the clips provide strong holding strength even in windy locations.
  • Will the clips damage or tear the shade net?
    No. The clip design distributes pressure evenly, reducing stress points and preventing net tearing.
