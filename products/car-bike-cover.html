Car & Bike Covers

High-quality protective covers designed to safeguard cars and bikes from dust, rain, sunlight, and environmental damage.

Product Overview

Car & Bike Covers are designed to provide reliable protection for vehicles against dust, rain, sunlight, bird droppings, and minor scratches. These covers help maintain the vehicle’s exterior finish and extend its lifespan.

Manufactured using durable and weather-resistant materials, these covers are suitable for both indoor and outdoor usage and are widely used in Indian residential and commercial parking environments.

Key Features

  • Protects vehicles from dust, rain, and UV exposure
  • Helps prevent scratches and surface damage
  • Lightweight yet durable construction
  • Easy to install, remove, and store
  • Available in car-specific and bike-specific sizes

Specifications

  • Material: Polyester / PE / Waterproof Fabric
  • Usage: Indoor and outdoor protection
  • Fit: Standard and customized sizes available
  • Colour: Grey, Silver, Blue
  • Protection: Dust, rain, sunlight, minor scratches

Applications & Use Cases

  • Personal cars and two-wheelers
  • Parking areas and residential complexes
  • Automobile showrooms and workshops
  • Long-term vehicle storage
  • Protection during non-use periods

Best Suited For

  • Vehicles parked in open or semi-covered areas
  • Daily-use cars and bikes exposed to dust and sunlight
  • Long-term parked vehicles in residential societies

Selection Note

For Indian conditions, waterproof covers are recommended during monsoon seasons, while breathable fabric is preferred for covered parking to prevent moisture buildup and paint damage.

Commonly Used Along With

Product FAQs

  • Are car and bike covers suitable for outdoor parking?
    Yes. Waterproof and UV-resistant covers are designed for outdoor use and protect vehicles from rain, sunlight, dust, and bird droppings.
  • Should the vehicle be completely dry before covering?
    Yes. The vehicle should be clean and dry before use to prevent moisture trapping and possible paint damage.
  • What type of cover is recommended during Indian monsoon?
    Fully waterproof covers are recommended during monsoon seasons, especially for vehicles parked in open areas.
  • Can covers cause scratches on vehicle paint?
    Properly fitted covers made from soft inner lining materials minimize scratching when used on clean vehicles.
  • Are custom-fit covers available for specific models?
    Yes. Vehicle-specific and customized size covers can be supplied for better fit and improved protection.
  • Are these covers suitable for long-term storage?
    Yes. Breathable covers are ideal for long-term storage to prevent moisture buildup while keeping dust and dirt away.
